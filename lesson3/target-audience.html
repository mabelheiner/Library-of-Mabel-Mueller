Target Audience

Persona No 1

I am a small business owner in Leroy. I am about 35 years of age. I have a couple of young kids in the community. I am looking for ways to get more word of about my business as well as fun activities for my kids.

Persona No 2

I am a stay at home mom who is new to the community. I am looking for places to volunteer and to take my kids out for fun. I like to be involved in my community and be surrounded by people that I know and who I care about.
