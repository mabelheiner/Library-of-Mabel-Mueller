Mabel Heiner

Color

Libenar

https://www.libenar.it/

The Libenar website provides product for babies. The color scheme is blue and pink which is a great strategy as it matches the audience of mothers who will think of their babies when they see these colors.

Repetition

EdApp

https://www.edapp.com/

The EdApp page uses repetition when you scroll down the home page and find various topics with links to learn more. Each link looks the same and has the same caption. This helps their audience know what to expect and helps the website to have a nice flow to it.

Visual Alignment

Audible Join Page

https://www.audible.com/

The Audible join page uses visual effects and alignment to draw potential subscribers into their website. The desktop version of their website also has lines of books pop up in the background which greatly pleases its users of book lovers and readers.
